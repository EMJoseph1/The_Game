THE GREATEST BATTLE EVER

Intructions: You must slay the big horn demon to reach enlightenment (and the treasure of course). Do a normal attack or charge up to do a special or Ultimate attack! Charge up once for a special or three times for an Ultimate! Don't forget you can heal if you are in a pinch but you are no doctor so be careful! Feel free to check your or your enemy's stats with the Stat buttton. Good luck, have fun and don't die!
